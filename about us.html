About Us

Our Story

Charis Technologies was founded in 2023 by Ms. Charity Mashele, a visionary entrepreneur passionate about combining technology and security to protect communities and businesses. Since its establishment, the company has grown rapidly, serving clients across Johannesburg and beyond.

Our Mission

To deliver tailored security and IT solutions that empower clients with safety, efficiency, and confidence in a connected world.

Our Vision

To shape the future of security and IT infrastructure by combining advanced technology with exceptional service. We aim to make safety and connectivity accessible to all, while creating safer communities and smarter workplaces through trusted systems that inspire confidence and peace of mind.

Our Values

  • Integrity in every project
  • Commitment to safety and realiability
  • Innovation and continuous improvemment
  • Customer-first approach

Meet Our Founder

Ms. Charity Mashele established Charis Technologies in 2023 with a vision to bridge the gap between advanced technology and everyday security needs.
